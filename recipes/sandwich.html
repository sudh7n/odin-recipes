Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet satisfying comfort food. With crispy golden bread and gooey melted cheese inside, itâ€™s the perfect quick meal for any time of the day. Pair it with tomato soup for a classic combo!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-low heat.
  4. Cook the sandwich for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
